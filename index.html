Bi-Monthly Unifying Scenario

Early CKD — A Shared Healthcare Response

Scenario: Mr. Ade, 52, presents with fatigue, swollen ankles, low urine output, and uncontrolled hypertension. His BP is 168/102 mmHg, and he has poor medication adherence. Early Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) is suspected.

How Each Member of the Health Ecosystem Responds

  • Clinician: Orders kidney tests, adjusts medication, and creates a plan.
  • Nurse: Reinforces adherence, monitors vitals, and educates on diet.
  • Medical Student: Takes history, performs exam, interprets labs.
  • Lab Scientist: Runs kidney markers accurately and flags abnormalities.
  • Allied Health: Dietitian, Pharmacist, Physiotherapist actions.
  • Public Health: Links case to community-level factors + awareness programs.
  • Health Manager: Ensures clinic systems, staff, supplies.
  • Caregiver: Supports adherence, diet, BP checks.
  • Software Developer: Builds apps for reminders & results tracking.

Every role matters. Every action protects a life. The health ecosystem needs you — just like Helpers Dynasty does.
