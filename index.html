Purpose & Mission

Helpers Dynasty International develops compassionate healthcare minds who extend kindness to communities, care for people in strategic areas, and foster friendship, fellowship and mutual understanding among members.

Helpers Dynasty — Structure & Branches

Each branch is a charter society — semi-autonomous, aligned to HD's mission.

HD Management

Governing body overseeing administration, coordination and growth across all branches and charter societies.

HD International

Global charter for members not part of a national society — virtual mentorship, research assistance and global study sessions.

HD Nigeria

Pioneer national chapter — expands into school-based branches and focuses on education, leadership and community projects.

HD Hope

Motivational arm — youth mentorship, scholarships, and social media motivational initiatives.

HD Sponsors

Financial & partnership division — works with donors and philanthropists to fund HD activities globally.

Core Activities

  • Study With Me Live Sessions — held three times a week from 7:00–10:00 PM EST.
  • Mentor–Mentee Pairing — connect members across fields for guidance.
  • Educational Resource Sharing — study materials and tools.
  • Research Assistance — collaboration & support for research projects.
  • Bi-monthly Saturday Hangouts — interactive community sessions.

Bi-Monthly Unifier — First Post

Scenario: Early CKD — A Team Response

Published: Nov 26, 2025

Scenario: Mr. Ade, 52, presents with fatigue, swollen ankles and reduced urine output. BP 168/102 mmHg. Suspect early chronic kidney disease. He often forgets medications and lacks a support system.

Clinician

Orders labs, adjusts meds, plans follow-up and educates the patient.

Nurse

Monitors vitals, trains on home BP checks and adherence support.

Medical Student

History, supervised exam, and clinical reasoning practice.

Lab Scientist

Runs and validates kidney function tests, flags abnormal results.

Allied Health

Diet, pharmacy checks and safe exercise plans.

Public Health

Designs community screening and awareness programs.

Health Manager

Ensures supplies, referral systems and appointment follow-up mechanisms.

Caregiver

Supports medication reminders, transport and emotional support.

Health Software Dev

Builds reminders, logging, and clinician-alert systems for eGFR/BP trends.

Unified message: Every role matters. Every action protects a life.

Membership

Membership is free and is formally granted after attending five (5) meetings and active participation. Members and non-members may raise funds — amounts are voluntary and demand-based.

Eligibility

  • Must be studying or have completed a program in a healthcare-related field.
  • Attend at least 5 meetings to become an official member.

Member Benefits

  • Official Certificate of Membership (downloadable via portal)
  • Access to mentorship, study groups, and leadership training
  • Eligibility to lead or participate in HD projects

Sponsor Benefits

  • Certificate of Sponsorship
  • Recognition on the website and in the annual report
  • Invitations to selected HD events
