About Us

Purpose & Mission:
To develop minds who extend kindness to their communities,
care for people in strategic areas, and
build friendship, fellowship, and mutual understanding among members.

Who We Are:
Helpers Dynasty MedEd is a student-led initiative dedicated to learning, mentorship, and personal growth in medicine.

Programs & Activities

  • Study With Me (7–10 PM daily): Join our live virtual study sessions.
  • Mentor–Mentee Pairing: Guidance for IUHS and other medical coursework.
  • Books & Materials Sharing: Access and share key medical resources.
  • Research Assistance: Available to close members.
  • Sunday Hangouts: Relaxed discussions about life and medicine.
