About Helpers Dynasty

Helpers Dynasty (HD), founded on 24th February 2017, is a global network of communities devoted to compassion, healthcare service, education, and leadership development. The movement inspires members to uplift vulnerable populations while building excellence in healthcare leadership worldwide.

Structure & Branches

1. HD Management

The global governing body that oversees all Helpers Dynasty chapters and charter societies worldwide. Ensures coordination, growth, and leadership in business, education, and community impact.

2. HD International

The global chapter for members without national societies. Provides virtual mentorship, academic support, research assistance, and global healthcare study initiatives.

3. HD Nigeria

The pioneer national chapter with expanding university and institutional clubs. Focuses on healthcare education, leadership training, and impactful community projects across Nigeria.

4. HD Hope

The motivational and youth empowerment branch. Responsible for mentorship programs, scholarships, and personal development campaigns.

5. HD Sponsors

The financial partnership and donor network supporting HD’s global activities. Helps fund projects, outreach programs, and international expansion.

Membership

Membership in HD is free, but granted only after:

  • Attendance of at least 5 meetings
  • Active participation in community or educational activities

Member Benefits

  • Certificate of Membership
  • Leadership and healthcare training
  • Eligibility to hold HD positions or lead branches
  • Access to the member portal
  • Networking with healthcare professionals globally

Non-Members

Non-members may also participate in many HD activities and may raise funds among themselves. Contributions remain flexible and demand-based.

For Sponsors & Donors

Sponsors receive recognition within the HD community, partnership certificates, and opportunities to support medical education, community outreach, and international humanitarian programs.
